Privacy Policy for our Apps

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal data is handled securely.

We do not collect any personal data directly. However, third-party services integrated into the App may collect data such as device information, usage data, and location data.

How We Use Your Information

We do not collect or store your personal data. Any information collected by third-party services is used to enhance your experience, such as improving app functionality and providing relevant content.

We do not share your personal data with third parties. However, third-party services may collect and share your data according to their privacy policies.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may choose to use the App without providing any personal data. You can also adjust your device's settings to limit data collection by third-party services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
